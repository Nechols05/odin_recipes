Who's ready for Lasagna?

This simple but delicious recipe is one of my husbands favorites. It's a nice hot dish to curb any comfort food cravings you might have.

I share this recipe in remebrance of Donna. The greatest Mother-in-law one could have. Ready to see why this is the lasagna to fall in love with? Check out the ingredients and recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 cans of three meat Ragu Sause
  • 1 box of lasagna noodles (cooked)
  • 1 container of cottage cheese
  • 1 container of ricotta chesse
  • 1 large bag of mozzarella chesse (shredded)
  • 1 container of parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsb of diced garlic

Directions

  1. Follow the instructions on the lasagna noodle box and cooked the noodles.
  2. Dice the onion and garlic. Once diced place in hot pan with some butter and saute
  3. Once the onions and garlic are cooked down and the onions apper translucent, remove from heat and place ina separte bowl.
  4. In the same pan, brown the ground beef.
  5. While the beef is cooking, combine the cottage and ricotta cheeses until completely mixed.
  6. Drain the grease from the meat. Place back in pan and return to heat.
  7. Add in sauce, onions, and garlic. Stir until fully combined. remove from heat.
  8. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  9. In a 9x13 glass baking dish it is time to start layering our ingredients.
  10. Start with a meat floor. Making sure to cover the whole bottom with anout 1/4 inch with the meat sause.
  11. Next, we are going to add a thin layer of our cottage and ricotta chesse mix
  12. After that layer, lay on some noodles. You may need to cut the to fit the pan. Thats ok, just cover everything the best you can.
  13. Now, we layer on the mozzarella.
  14. From here you can repeat the meat and cheese mix noodles more chesse. Once you get to the very top layer of noodles, put what is left of your meat sauce on top of the noodles.
  15. Finally, top with questional amounts of cheese.
  16. Cover with tinfoil.
  17. Place in over for about 35 minutes.
  18. Remove from over and remove the tinfoil.
  19. Place back in oven for another 10 minutes.
