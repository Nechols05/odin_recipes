No-Bake Cheesecake Recipe

Back to Home

You won't need to turn your oven on to enjoy this creamy sweet treat. No-bake cheesecake can be made with dozens of flavors to suit your cravings tonight.

This easy no-bake cheesecake is my go-to recipe when I want cheesecake in a flash. It's as easy as 1-2-3! Top with additional graham cracker crumbs and whipped topping, if desired. I love to share this with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 oz) packages of cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 3 Tbls of vanilla extract. This can be done to taste as well.
  • 1 tsp of lemon extract (optional)
  • 1 (8 oz) container of frozen whipped topping (cool whip), thawed
  • 1 (9-inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 (21 oz) can of your choice of pie filling. My family likes cherry and strawberry

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, blend the cream cheese, vanilla extract, and lemon extract together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Slowly fold in the whipped cream, be sure to scrape the sides and swap directions of your folds.
  3. Roll your crust out in to the 9 inch glass round baking dish.
  4. Pour your cream cheese mixture into the crust and smooth over.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  6. Top with your pie filling and crushed up graham crackers.

This is one of those make in the morning and enjoy after dinner. I hope you try it and I hope you like it.
